Resolve debt for less using PaydOff

PaydOff is not a settlement company, law firm or a loan. It is an innovative approach that allows you to affordably resolve your debt without the big price tag of a professional service. You are in control of all decisions about your debt; UGotiate is there to guide you every step of the way. Follow the steps below and you will be on your way to being debt free!

Now… How Exactly Does UGotiate Work?

Get Started

In a few easy steps you can be a UGotiator! You'll soon be negotiating with creditors to resolve debts for less than you owe, without having to hire a professional service

Create a Plan

You are in control of your money. You decide what you can afford to pay back and over how long. UGotiate's interactive Debt O Meter shows you how realistic your plan is to resolve all of you debt.

Guidance every step

Your personalized "To Do List" keeps you on track towards successful resolutions. The "UGuidance Central" shows you how to effectively handle the most common events when resolving debts.

Save up money

Set up a dedicated bank account for resolution funds. Charts & tracking in UGotiate monitor your money. As funds accumulate, UGotiate alerts you when you may have enough to start negotiating.

Negotiate Effectively!

UGotiate provides alerts when it may be time to make an offer. We'll guide through making offers and negotiating to successfully get deals done. It's like having an experienced negotiator in your corner

Become Debt Free!

As each debt is resolved, you are closer to reaching your goal. UGotiate monitors your progress and guides you every step of the way until your last account is resolved. With UGotiate you'll soon learn that nothing feels as good as being debt free!
